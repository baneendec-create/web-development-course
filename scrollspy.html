Section 1

Codingal

Coding, or computer programming, is the process of writing sets of instructions in specialized languages (like Python, Java, or C++) that tell computers and electronic devices how to perform specific tasks. It acts as a bridge between human logic and machine action, enabling everything from web design and app development to controlling, robotics, and cars. By creating precise, logical algorithms—essentially recipes for the computer—programmers enable software to manage data, display visuals, and automate daily tasks

Codingal

Coding, or computer programming, is the process of writing sets of instructions in specialized languages (like Python, Java, or C++) that tell computers and electronic devices how to perform specific tasks. It acts as a bridge between human logic and machine action, enabling everything from web design and app development to controlling, robotics, and cars. By creating precise, logical algorithms—essentially recipes for the computer—programmers enable software to manage data, display visuals, and automate daily tasks
